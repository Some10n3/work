## **LeSS (Large-Scale Scrum)**  

ğŸ”¹ **What is LeSS?**  
- A **scaled-up version of Scrum** for **large projects** with multiple teams.  
- Introduced by **Craig Larman & Bas Vodde (2005)**.  

ğŸ”¹ **How LeSS Works?**  
âœ… **One Product Backlog** for all teams.  
âœ… **One Product Owner** manages the backlog.  
âœ… **Multiple teams working in the same Sprint**.  
âœ… **One Definition of Done (DoD) for all teams**.

- **Overall Retrospective** â€“ Scrum Masters, PO, and team reps discuss **improvements**.
